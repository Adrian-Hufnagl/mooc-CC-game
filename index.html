Colors & Clothing

Twins - Dress & Describe - minimum 2 Players

For a party, schoolmates decide to dress alike to look like identical twins. Choose a role you want to play.

The Dresser: Choose a figure and paint its clothes. Describe the coloured character to the Copycat by using the right vocabulary.
The Copycat: While listening to the description of the Dresser, you try to match the figures' clothes and colours.
In the end, the Dresser's and Copycat's characters should look the same.
